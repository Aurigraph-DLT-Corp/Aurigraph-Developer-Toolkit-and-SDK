## TIPS FOR EFFECTIVE DAILY REPORTING

```
âœ“ DO:
  - Be specific with numbers and percentages
  - Keep executive summary to 1-2 sentences
  - Escalate early, don't wait for problem to get worse
  - Use consistent format daily for easy tracking
  - Include both positives and concerns
  - Provide clear next steps/priorities
  - Send by 5:15 PM - don't delay communication

âœ— DON'T:
  - Use vague language ("going pretty well")
  - Omit blockers in hopes they'll go away
  - Send report 2+ hours late
  - Change template format mid-week
  - Over-explain details (link to full docs)
  - Include emotional reactions
  - Make excuses instead of focusing on fixes
```
