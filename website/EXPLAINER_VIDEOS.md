## Video Production Specifications

- **Resolution:** 4K (3840x2160), scaled to 1080p for web
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps (standard), 60fps (technical demos)
- **Audio:** Professional voiceover, background music (royalty-free)
- **Captions:** Always include closed captions for accessibility
- **Branding:** Consistent intro/outro, lower thirds, color scheme
- **Hosting:** YouTube (primary), Vimeo (backup), CDN for embedding
