## Overview

The Corporate & Finance Lawyer skill provides specialized guidance on corporate structuring, securities law, financing arrangements, mergers and acquisitions, and corporate governance. This skill covers entity formation, capital raising, debt structuring, equity issuance, and board-level governance for blockchain companies.

## Core Expertise Areas

### 1. Corporate Structure & Formation
- **Entity Selection**: C-corp, S-corp, LLC, Delaware C-corp benefits
- **Multi-Subsidiary Structure**: Holding company architecture
- **International Structure**: Tax treaties, transfer pricing, base erosion
- **Regulatory Approval**: Special purpose acquisition companies (SPACs)
- **Incorporation Documents**: Articles, bylaws, operating agreements
- **Governance Framework**: Board structure, shareholder agreements

### 2. Capital Raising & Financing
- **Equity Financing**: Series A/B/C rounds, strategic rounds
- **Debt Financing**: Term loans, convertible debt, bonds
- **Hybrid Instruments**: Convertible notes, SAFEs, tokens
- **Valuation**: Fair value determination, 409A valuations
- **Investor Rights**: Liquidation preferences, anti-dilution
- **Due Diligence**: Financial, legal, and regulatory diligence

### 3. Securities Law
- **Reg D Private Placement**: Accredited investor verification
- **Reg A+ Offering**: Smaller IPO alternative
- **Reg S Offshore Offering**: Non-US investor sales
- **Rule 506 Safe Harbor**: Solicitation and bad actor rules
- **Intrastate Exemptions**: State-limited offerings
- **Token Classification**: Security vs. utility determination

### 4. Mergers & Acquisitions
- **Transaction Structure**: Stock vs. asset purchase
- **Due Diligence Process**: Financial, tax, legal reviews
- **Representations & Warranties**: Seller guarantees
- **Earnout & Escrow**: Post-closing adjustment mechanisms
- **Integration Planning**: Business combination procedures
- **Regulatory Approval**: Antitrust and sector-specific review

### 5. Corporate Governance
- **Board Structure**: Director selection, committees, independence
- **Shareholder Rights**: Voting, inspection, derivative suits
- **Officer Duties**: Fiduciary duties, standard of care
- **Conflicts of Interest**: Related party transactions, disclosure
- **Compliance Framework**: Corporate records, minutes, resolutions
- **Whistleblower Protection**: Anonymous reporting procedures

### 6. Debt Financing & Credit Agreements
- **Term Loan Structure**: Maturity, interest, covenants
- **Credit Facilities**: Revolving, bridge, equipment financing
- **Covenant Monitoring**: Financial, operational, event-based
- **Default Procedures**: Acceleration, remedies, workout
- **Subordination**: Intercreditor agreements and lien priority
- **Refinancing Strategies**: Rate optimization, maturity management

### 7. Equity & Cap Table Management
- **Stock Option Plans**: 409A valuation, vesting schedules
- **Restricted Stock Units (RSUs)**: Vesting and tax treatment
- **Cap Table Management**: Dilution and ownership tracking
- **Investor Preferences**: Liquidation, anti-dilution, conversion
- **Drag-Along & Tag-Along**: Minority shareholder rights
- **Right of First Refusal**: Secondary sale restrictions

### 8. Strategic Partnerships & JVs
- **Joint Venture Formation**: Ownership, governance, economics
- **Partnership Agreements**: Contribution, profit sharing, exit
- **Strategic Alliances**: Collaboration without equity
- **Technology Licensing**: IP licensing and commercialization
- **Distribution Agreements**: Channel partner relationships
- **Co-Development Arrangements**: Shared R&D and ownership
