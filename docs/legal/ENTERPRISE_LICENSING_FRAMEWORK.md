## Part 1: Licensing Model Overview

### 1.1 License Types & Use Cases

**Type 1: Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) License**
- **Target**: Enterprises needing managed blockchain infrastructure
- **Users**: DeFi protocols, RWA platforms, enterprise consortiums
- **Deployment**: Hosted on Aurigraph infrastructure
- **Pricing**: Per-TPS subscription model
- **Features**: Managed nodes, consensus participation, governance access
- **Typical Users**: 5-50 per customer
- **SLA**: 99.95% uptime, <500ms finality

**Type 2: Self-Hosted Enterprise License**
- **Target**: Regulated enterprises, financial institutions, governments
- **Users**: Banks, insurance companies, central banks
- **Deployment**: Customer-hosted on-premises or private cloud
- **Pricing**: Perpetual with annual maintenance
- **Features**: Full source access, customization rights, dedicated support
- **Typical Users**: 100-1,000 per customer
- **SLA**: 99.99% uptime, 24/7 support response

**Type 3: Technology License (IP Licensing)**
- **Target**: Blockchain infrastructure companies, tech firms
- **Users**: Protocol developers, infrastructure providers
- **Deployment**: Integrated into customer's platform
- **Pricing**: Upfront + per-transaction royalties
- **Features**: HyperRAFT++, quantum crypto, AI optimization algorithms
- **Typical Uses**: Consensus algorithms, cryptographic implementations
- **Royalty Rate**: 0.5-2% of transaction fees

**Type 4: OEM/Reseller License**
- **Target**: System integrators, blockchain vendors
- **Users**: Hardware manufacturers, software platforms
- **Deployment**: White-label or integrated solutions
- **Pricing**: Volume-based with tiered discounts
- **Features**: Customizable branding, integration APIs, resale rights
- **Typical Resellers**: 10-50 per program
- **Revenue Share**: 20-40% to reseller

**Type 5: Developer Ecosystem License**
- **Target**: Dapp developers, open-source contributors
- **Users**: Individual developers, startups (<$10M revenue)
- **Deployment**: Testnet or public blockchain access
- **Pricing**: Free tier + premium features ($99-999/month)
- **Features**: API access, developer tools, technical support
- **Typical Users**: 100-500+ per tier
- **SLA**: Best-effort support

**Type 6: Academic & Government License**
- **Target**: Universities, research institutions, government agencies
- **Users**: Academic researchers, policy makers
- **Deployment**: Non-commercial infrastructure access
- **Pricing**: Free or significantly discounted
- **Features**: Source access for research, publication rights
- **Typical Users**: 10-20 per institution
- **SLA**: Community support

## Part 8: Legal & Compliance

### 8.1 Regulatory Considerations

**Export Control**:
- Quantum-resistant cryptography (EAR subject)
- Customer screening against denied parties lists
- License request to BIS for restricted countries
- Compliance team review for ≥$500K deals

**Data Protection (GDPR/CCPA)**:
- Aurigraph acts as processor (customer is controller)
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) required
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfer
- 72-hour breach notification

**Sanctions & AML/KYC**:
- KYC required for ≥$100K deals
- Enhanced due diligence for ≥$1M deals
- OFAC, SDN, Entity List screening
