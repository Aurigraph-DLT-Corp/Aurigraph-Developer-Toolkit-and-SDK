**Blockchain Transactions are Permanent and Immutable**:
- Once a transaction is recorded on the blockchain, **it cannot be reversed, modified, or deleted**

**Financial Consequences**:

- We are not liable for transactions sent to incorrect addresses

**Your Responsibility**:
- You are responsible for all transaction verification
- Double-check recipient addresses before sending

### 5.2 Transaction Processing

**Transaction Submission**:
- Transactions are submitted to the network in the order received
- Network congestion may delay processing
- We do not guarantee transaction ordering or priority
- Transactions are final once recorded on the blockchain

**Transaction Fees**:
- You agree to pay applicable transaction fees
- Fees are non-refundable even if transaction fails
- Fee structure may change without notice
- Actual fees depend on network congestion and transaction size

**Failed Transactions**:
- Network conditions may cause transaction failures
- Some failures are not refundable (e.g., out-of-gas on EVM-compatible chains)
- Failed transactions cannot be reversed after confirmation
- We are not liable for failed transaction fees

### 5.3 Network Consensus

Transactions are final when:
- The transaction is recorded on the Aurigraph blockchain
- Confirmation blocks have been produced
- Consensus finality has been achieved (~X blocks, ~X seconds)

**Before Finality**:
- Transaction may be reversed if network re-organizes
- Do not consider transaction final until finality achieved
- Wait for confirmation notifications

**After Finality**:
- Transaction is **PERMANENTLY recorded** and immutable
- Cannot be reversed under any circumstances
- Cannot be modified or deleted
- Will remain on blockchain indefinitely (10+ years, likely forever)
