### 6.1 Cookie Policy

We use cookies for the following purposes:

**Cookies You Can Control**:
- Marketing cookies (can be rejected via cookie banner)
- Analytics cookies (can be rejected via cookie banner)
- Functional cookies (can be disabled in browser settings)

**Cookies That Are Essential**:

- CSRF protection (cannot be disabled)

### 6.2 Cookie Management

**In Your Browser**:
- Most browsers allow you to delete or block cookies
- This may affect Website functionality
- Instructions vary by browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)

**On Our Website**:
- Click the Cookie Settings button
- Choose which cookie categories to accept
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Your preferences saved for 12 months

**Do Not Track**:
- Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" signal
- We respect Do Not Track preferences by:
  - Not using marketing cookies
  - Not implementing Hotjar recordings
  - Continuing to use essential cookies (required for functionality)

### 6.3 Third-Party Cookies

Third parties on our Website may set their own cookies:
- These are governed by their privacy policies

- You can manage third-party cookies via your browser

**Third-Party Cookie Providers**:

## 7. DATA RETENTION

### 7.1 Retention Periods

**Contact Form Submissions**:
- Retention: 2 years (or until inquiry resolved + 1 year)
- Purpose: Responding to inquiries and potential follow-up
- Deletion: Automatically deleted after 2 years

**Email Subscribers**:
- Retention: Duration of subscription
- Deletion: Immediate upon unsubscribe
- Unsubscribe: One-click unsubscribe in email footer

**Website Analytics Data**:
- Retention: 26 months (Google Analytics default)
- Purpose: Analyzing traffic patterns and user behavior
- Deletion: Automatic per Google Analytics policy

**Session Data**:
- Retention: 24 hours (for active sessions only)
- Purpose: Maintaining login and shopping cart
- Deletion: Automatically expires after inactivity

**Cookies**:
- Retention: Varies by type
  - Session cookies: Until browser closed
  - Persistent cookies: 1-2 years
  - Analytics: Per third-party policy
- Deletion: You can delete via browser settings

**Hotjar Recordings**:
- Retention: 360 days
- Deletion: Automatically deleted after 1 year
- Anonymization: User identifiers anonymized after 30 days

**Payment Information**:
- Retention: Per PCI compliance (usually 1-3 years)
- Note: We don't store credit cards; processor handles
- Deletion: Per payment processor's retention policy

## 10. SECURITY OF YOUR INFORMATION

### 10.1 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures:
- **Encryption**: TLS 1.3 for data in transit; AES-256 for sensitive data
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Firewalls**: Web application firewall (WAF) protection
- **Monitoring**: Intrusion detection and threat monitoring
- **Testing**: Regular security audits and penetration testing
- **Backups**: Regular backups for disaster recovery

### 10.2 Limitations

No security system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee:
- Complete protection against all attacks
- Unauthorized access to all information
- Security of information you transmit over public networks

### 10.3 Breach Notification

If we discover a breach affecting your Website data:

- Notification will include breach details and recommended actions
