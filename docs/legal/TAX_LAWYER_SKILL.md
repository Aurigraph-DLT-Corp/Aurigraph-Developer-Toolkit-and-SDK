## Core Expertise Areas

### 1. Cryptocurrency Tax Classification
- **US Tax Treatment**: IRS Notice 2014-21, Rev. Proc. 2022-14
- **Virtual Currency Classification**: Property vs. currency treatment
- **Token Types**: Utility tokens, security tokens, stablecoins
- **Tax Basis Calculation**: Cost basis determination and tracking
- **Identification Methods**: FIFO, LIFO, specific identification, average cost

### 2. Token Economics & Incentive Design
- **Tokenomics Planning**: Supply mechanics, emission schedules, burn rates
- **Reward Structuring**: Staking rewards, LP incentives, airdrops, referrals
- **Tax Optimization**: Structuring rewards to minimize tax burden
- **Governance Tokens**: Tax treatment of governance rights and voting
- **Liquidity Mining**: Yield farming rewards and capital gains mechanics

### 3. Capital Gains Taxation
- **Short-term Gains**: Ordinary income rates (US federal 37% max)
- **Long-term Gains**: Preferential rates (15-20% for high earners)
- **Wash Sale Rules**: Timing considerations for token sales
- **Like-kind Exchanges**: Section 1031 treatment (limited post-2017)
- **Tracking Requirements**: Wash sale tracking and reporting

### 4. Income Tax Categories
- **Ordinary Income**: Staking rewards, mining rewards, airdrop rewards
- **Capital Gains**: Token appreciation on sale or exchange
- **Dividend-like Income**: Revenue sharing, protocol rewards
- **Foreign Earned Income**: FEIE exclusion for digital nomads
- **Self-employment Tax**: 15.3% for independent token operators

### 5. International Tax Compliance
- **OECD Framework**: Common Reporting Standard (CRS)
- **US Citizens Abroad**: FATCA filing requirements
- **Country-Specific Rules**:
  - EU: VAT treatment, CRS reporting
  - UK: Capital gains tax, ISA eligibility considerations
  - Singapore: GST treatment, tax residency
  - Hong Kong: Profits tax, no capital gains tax
  - Switzerland: Cantonal treatment of crypto

### 6. Entity Structure Optimization
- **Corporate Structures**: C-corp, S-corp, LLC, partnership
- **Tax Residency**: Jurisdiction selection for tax neutrality
- **Permanent Establishment**: Avoiding PE risk in operations
- **Transfer Pricing**: Intercompany pricing for multi-entity operations
- **Holding Companies**: Asset protection and tax deferral

### 7. Cross-Border Transactions
- **Withholding Taxes**: Country-specific withholding requirements
- **Treaty Benefits**: US tax treaties reducing withholding
- **Controlled Foreign Corporations**: CFC rules for offshore entities
- **Global Intangible Low-Taxed Income**: GILTI provisions
- **Subpart F Income**: Passive income from foreign corporations

### 8. DeFi & Advanced Tax Issues
- **Impermanent Loss**: Tax treatment of LP rebalancing losses
- **Yield Farming**: Treatment as ordinary income vs. capital gains
- **Flash Loans**: Tax implications of atomic swaps and flash deals
- **Airdrops**: Ordinary income at fair market value on receipt
- **Fork Coins**: Tax treatment of cryptocurrency hard forks
- **Wrapped Tokens**: Bridge tokens and tax basis concerns

### 9. Tax-Loss Harvesting Strategies
- **Systematic Loss Taking**: Planned loss realization
- **Wash Sale Planning**: 30-day windows and timing
- **Like-Kind Exchanges**: Section 1031 planning (limited)
- **Basis Step-Up**: Strategic holding for death benefit
- **Charitable Giving**: Donation of appreciated tokens

### 10. Reporting Obligations
- **Form 8949**: Sales of Capital Assets
- **Schedule D**: Capital Gains and Losses summary
- **Form 1040**: Individual income tax return
- **FinCEN Form 114 (FBAR)**: Foreign account reporting
- **Form 3520/3520-A**: Foreign trust reporting
- **Form 5471**: CFC reporting requirements
- **Form 8938**: FATCA reporting threshold

## Token Economics Framework

### Token Emission Schedule Planning
- **Fixed Supply**: Deflationary mechanics and scarcity value
- **Inflationary**: Annual emission rate and inflation impact
- **Diminishing Emission**: Halving schedules (Bitcoin-style)
- **Burn Mechanics**: Token destruction and supply reduction
- **Community Incentives**: Airdrop and reward schedules

### Reward Structure Design
| Reward Type | Tax Treatment | Reporting |
|---|---|---|
| **Staking** | Ordinary income | Form 1099-MISC |
| **Mining** | Ordinary income | Form 1099-MISC |
| **Airdrop** | Ordinary income (FMV) | Form 1099-NEC |
| **LP Fees** | Capital gains + ordinary | Mixed reporting |
| **Governance** | Capital gains (if holding) | Schedule D |
| **Referral** | Ordinary income | 1099-NEC or business income |

### Tokenomics Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Define token supply and emission schedule

- [ ] Optimize for tax efficiency by holder type
- [ ] Plan founder/team token vesting (for capital gains)
- [ ] Design community distribution program
- [ ] Create tax documentation templates
- [ ] Plan for token exchange and trading taxation
