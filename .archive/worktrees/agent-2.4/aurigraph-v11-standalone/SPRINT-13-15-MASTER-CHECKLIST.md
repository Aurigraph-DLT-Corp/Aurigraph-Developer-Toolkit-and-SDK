- Contact: DevOps Lead
- Response Time: < 2 hours
- Escalate if: Service outage

**Timeline & Scope Questions**:
- Contact: Project Manager
- Response Time: < 1 hour
- Escalate if: Risk to schedule

### Escalation Levels

**Level 1** (Yellow Alert):
- Single component affected
- Workaround available
- No schedule impact
- **Action**: Investigate and resolve within 24 hours

**Level 2** (Orange Alert):
- Multiple components affected
- No workaround available
- Potential schedule impact
- **Action**: Escalate to Frontend Lead, resolve within 4 hours

**Level 3** (Red Alert):
- Sprint-level impact
- Blocking multiple developers
- Schedule at risk
- **Action**: Emergency standup, escalate to PM, resolve ASAP
