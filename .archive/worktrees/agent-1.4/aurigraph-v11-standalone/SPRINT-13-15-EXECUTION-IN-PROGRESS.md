## üö® ESCALATION & BLOCKER MANAGEMENT

### Blocker Levels

**YELLOW (Low Priority)**
- Single component affected
- Workaround available
- No schedule impact
- **Action**: Resolve within 24 hours

**ORANGE (Medium Priority)**
- Multiple components affected
- No workaround available
- Potential schedule impact
- **Action**: Escalate to Frontend Lead, resolve within 4 hours

**RED (Critical)**
- Sprint-level impact
- Blocking multiple developers
- Schedule at risk
- **Action**: Emergency escalation, resolve ASAP

### Escalation Contacts

| Level | Contact | Response | Method |
|-------|---------|----------|--------|
| Yellow | Developer team | 24 hours | Standup |
| Orange | Frontend Lead | 4 hours | Slack/Standup |
| Red | Project Manager | Immediate | Emergency |
